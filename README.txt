A Danmaku Prototype Remake with Unity
Ryan Cai (xcai30@ucsc.edu)

Objective - survive as long as you can! Getting hit by either the bullet or the enemy will crash!

Control - W,A,S,D for 8 way movements
	- Left-click to fire a bullet

Assets - all sound made by me using Bfxr
